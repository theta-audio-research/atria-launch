ATRIA

Performance-Driven Creative FX Instrument

ATRIA is an instrument: immediate, playable, and emotionally reactive. Designed around the principle “Shape motion. Not parameters,” ATRIA replaces the effect rack with a three-ring macro system that redefines control as musical intent. The Macro Performance System is organized into three dimensions: • Left: Time. Governing drift, timing instability, and playback displacement. • Center: Motion. The groove dimension: transient emphasis, rhythmic energy, and spread. • Right: Energy. Physical impact: harmonic density, pressure, and saturation. Linked meta-macros extend this behavior: Flow Link ties inner rings for rhythmic energy, while Form Link ties outer rings for tonal and spatial reshaping. Visually, ATRIA follows the THETA “Void” palette with dedicated module families: Teal (Transient/Warble), Coral (Drive/Crush), and Purple (Modulation/Output). It is intentionally layered so the user always begins with the rings. Contextual controls appear only when needed, ensuring instinct always leads over engineering.

PERFORMANCE_ENGINE

  • Transient Shaping Pulse-shaping front end for immediate percussive articulation.
  • Rhythmic Gating Dual Stutter stages (A/B) for timing instability and animated pulse.

  • Digital Degradation Bit/Rate reduction stage for fragmented textural movement.
  • Tape Drift Wow and flutter modeling for temporal instability and tonal wear.
