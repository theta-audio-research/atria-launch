EROSION

Four-Stage Saturation Engine

EROSION treats degradation as a physical process. Built around four distinct analog circuit models, it provides a flexible routing architecture paired with a performance-grade XY control surface. From invisible warmth to aggressive destruction, EROSION covers the full spectrum of nonlinear transformation in a single insert. Whether used for subtle mood layering or full signal collapse, EROSION steers sound through a signal path built for technical beauty and controlled failure.
