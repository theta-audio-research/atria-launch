ARECIBO is a cinematic sound-transformation plugin from THETA Audio Research. At its core is a layered source engine and motion chain that moves through THETA Source Array → DRIFT → BREATHE → FIELD → FADE → PAN. The architecture is built around pre-QUORRA tone shaping, the QUORRA motion engine, and a final Wall Stage for transient control and limiting. Use it to create spectral movement, unstable harmonics, evolving texture, and cinematic transformation across a single continuous chain. ARECIBO does not just color sound — it steers it.
