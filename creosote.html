Not eight presets of one compressor — eight true hardware topologies.

SUMMING & STAGING — The Console Bus

Character saturation, stereo glue, and a ceiling clipper — all anti-derivative anti-aliased (ADAA), all measured.

  • · Summing — PREGAIN, WIDTH (M/S), BALANCE. The last analog bus before the wide world.
  • · IRON — A modeled output transformer (off / subtle / rich) with three voicings (British console, American punch, broadcast desk) built as a flux integrator with magnetic remanence, not an EQ curve.
  • · Staging — Honest INPUT/LEVEL staging. Limit lookahead ceiling pouring oil-damped reduction top-down. AUTO LEVEL for one-button unity out.
  • · TONE — A direction-aware tilt. Darkening reaches into the presence region to warm rather than dull. Brightening lifts the air band and tightens lows.

Unit Variance

No two racks alike

Real hardware never matched. CREOSOTE models that: pick UNIT 1 through 16 and the instance adopts that unit's component tolerances — filter corners drift up to ±5%, drive points shift fractions of a dB. Deterministic, recall-safe, and loudness-matched to ±0.005 dB.

Punch Protection

Saturate the sustain

Heavy drive usually costs you your transients. PUNCH detects onsets and momentarily eases the drive into the saturators (level-compensated, wet-path only) so the crack of the stick passes clean while the body stays thick. Measured: attacks pass ~1.4 dB cleaner at FULL.

Photographed UI

The Console

Not a picture of a console — it's a console, photographed. Panels, caps, and instruments rendered in Blender Cycles under one locked studio light. Real bulb-backlit VUs, chunky bronze selectors, and light that spills organically across the plate.

Session Safety

Versioned DSP

Every audible improvement in CREOSOTE ships behind a DSP version gate. A session saved on any previous version recalls bit-identical while new sessions get the newest behavior. Enforced with an automated 36-pass-bar audio regression suite on every build.
