THE_OVERVIEW

Named for the heavy particles that make up the mass of ordinary matter, the name is the brief: where a normal EQ can only boost what's already there, Baryon creates what isn't. Feed it a thin kick, a bass that disappears on small speakers, an 808 that won't sit, or a full mix that needs a reinforced foundation — and it builds a controlled, musical sub that breathes with the performance instead of booming over it.

THE_SUBHARMONIC_ENGINE

THE THREE-BAND ENGINE
ENGINE_BANDS_PENDING

At the heart of Baryon is a three-band generator. Each band is independently tunable so you can target the exact region you want to reinforce and leave the rest alone, tracking three core generation philosophies:

  • · DIVIDE — The classic hardware divider approach. The source band is squared and flip-flopped on every zero crossing to yield a waveform exactly one octave down, smoothed into a fat, gritty, rhythmically-locked sub layer.
  • · SYNTH — Clean waveform modeling. Tracks the dominant pitch in the band and drives a continuous-phase sine oscillator at one octave, a fifth (÷3), or two octaves (÷4) below. Pure and artifact-free.
  • · HYBRID — Blends the grit and transient attack of the divider seamlessly with the body and weight of the sine oscillator.
ENVELOPE AND HARMONIC SCULPTING

The generated sub tracks the envelope of your source to swell and decay naturally with the performance. A built-in bass gate blocks tracking on low-level rumble or signal bleed. Tone shursh parameters include:

  • · Tight Punch — A resonant high-pass filter that adds focused impact at the very bottom boundary.
  • · Sub-Tone Control — Dials weight dynamically from round and subby to highly present and aggressive.
  • · Per-Engine Saturation — Adds dedicated upper-harmonics so your low-end updates stay perfectly legible on smaller headphones and mobile speaker nodes. Features multiple tracking curves paired with an inverse "round" mode.
DUAL DYNAMIC EQ — INPUT AND OUTPUT
EQ_MATRIX_PENDING

Baryon gives you two complete EQ stages: one to clean and prep the source before generation, and one to polish the result after. Both feature steep 24 dB/octave high- and low-pass filters for surgical band-limiting, plus a true dynamic bell filter. Each bell carries static gain alongside an independent dynamic range to react like a frequency-conscious compressor.

Draggable Curve Modifier Logic:

  • · Drag Node — Direct calibration of Frequency and Gain.
  • · ⌘-Drag — Surgical scale adjustments of Bandwidth (Q).
  • · ⌥-Drag — Independent tracking of localized Dynamic Range metrics.
  • · Shift-Click — Solos and auditions the specific frequency band instantly.
DYNAMICS & HARDWARE CHARACTER

Once the subharmonic matrix is compiled and shaped, Baryon targets the final output block with a dedicated processing chain:

  • · RMS Compressor — Smooth, musical tracking in the spirit of the classic dbx 160 compressor. Complete with threshold, soft knee, ratio (up to infinity), attack/release, auto-release, makeup, parallel mix, and an input drive stage for extra grit.
  • · Harmonic Saturation — Six selectable shaping curves (Tanh, Arctan, Tube, Tape, Diode, and Hard clip) plus an inverse "round" mode for warmth. Linked with adjustable drive, mix, and 2× / 4× oversampling tiers for clean, alias-free harmonics.
  • · Brickwall Limiter — A transparent look-ahead limiter with adjustable ceiling, release, look-ahead times, and an optional clip stage to clamp down on final output peak transients.
