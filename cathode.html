CATHODE

Character Processor and Creative Degradation Instrument

RETURN_TO_LAB

CATHODE treats degradation as one coherent system. Instead of generic lo-fi modules, it operates on the premise that the sound is the transmission, and the plugin is the unstable machine carrying it. At the core of CATHODE is a signal path built around heat, instability, interruption, and control. It can add tube-like weight and asymmetry, induce worn-playback movement, capture fractured glitch states, and push material into forms of failure that still feel musical. What sets CATHODE apart is the identity of its modules: • Receiver: head wear, lock stress, AGC breathing, and contact failure. • Phosphor: thermal heat, fatigue, bloom, and monitor chemistry. • Interference: rival signals, takeover moments, and static seizure.
