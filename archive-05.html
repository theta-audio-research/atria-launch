PLANETARY_RESONANCES

ABSTRACT: Cataloging the low-frequency "hums" generated by planetary rotation and magnetosphere friction.

THE INVISIBLE HUM

Every celestial body possesses a fundamental resonance—a hum that exists below the threshold of human hearing until shifted up via the ARECIBO reception array. These are not just noises; they are the acoustic thumbprints of distant worlds.

MAPPING_FIELD

Mapping the 'Field' parameter in the plugin allows the user to sweep through these planetary layers, moving from the metallic ring of gas giants to the hollow, percussive silence of rocky moons.
