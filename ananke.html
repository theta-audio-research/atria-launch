DESIGN_PHILOSOPHY

Under the hood is a modern feed-forward compressor with a smooth, program-dependent decoupled peak detector and a soft-knee gain computer. Detection is stereo-linked — one gain reduction value is applied across both channels, so ANANKE clamps down hard without ever shifting or smearing the stereo image. It runs in mono or stereo, and the engine is mono-first by design: predictable, surgical, and transparent when you want it to be.

At its default settings, with drive at zero, ANANKE is bit-transparent — a clean, uncoloured compressor. Everything beyond that is a deliberate choice you reach for.

CHASSIS_CONTROLS

VOICE — Three Dynamics Characters

In Greek myth, Ananke is the primordial force of necessity and constraint — the inevitability that holds everything else in its place. It's a fitting name for a compressor: the quiet discipline that keeps a performance under control.

A single control maps the ballistics behavior of the compressor, decoupled completely from any harmonic coloration curves:

  • · CLEAN — Faithful, transparent gain reduction. The compressor, out of the way.
  • · WARM — Rounder, more forgiving dynamics that ease onto the signal timeline gracefully.
  • · PUNCH — Faster, transient-forward tracking behavior that prioritizes snap and energy up front.
CHARACTER — Saturation without clipping

The coloration network is entirely decoupled from the dynamics engine, allowing you to dial in tone and harmonics completely independent of attenuation depth. Includes three analogue-modeled voicings:

  • · TUBE — Even-harmonic warmth, weight, and a soft, rounded top.
  • · DIODE — A grittier blend of even and odd harmonics with a controlled high end response.
  • · BRIGHT — A lean, odd-harmonic edge that keeps air and high-frequency bite intact.
  • · High-Frequency Softening — The DRIVE control adds rich harmonic weight without digital harshness. Built-in post-saturation softening dynamically tracks the drive to clip out brittle, fizzy upper overtones. Coloration routing can be toggled pre or post compression.
DETECTION AND PARALLEL BLENDING

Surgically fit the compression envelope to any dialogue or mix material with deep circuit controls:

  • · Detector Blend — Sweep continuously from lightning-fast peak-accurate detection to smooth RMS tracking.
  • · Auto-Release — An optical, program-dependent release that breathes organically with source performance.
  • · Sidechain High-Pass — Prevents low-frequency anchors from triggering threshold attenuation, eliminating low-end pumping.
  • · Range Floor — Sets a hard ceiling on total gain reduction to prevent over-compression.
  • · Fixed-Budget Lookahead — Offers up to 3.5 ms of lookahead to catch fast transients. Budgeted latency remains fixed, avoiding plug-in delay re-sync artifacts during automation.
  • · Parallel Wet/Dry Mix — Full internal blending for instant New York-style parallel compression.
LOUDNESS METERING & ANALYSIS

Features an integrated ITU-R BS.1770-4 loudness meter directly on the output path. Level stems, full dialogue passes, or instrument mixes straight to EBU R128, streaming, or broadcast delivery specifications in real time without external analyzers.
