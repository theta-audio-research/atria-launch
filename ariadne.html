STEREO DELAY FIELD

Tempo-syncable delay architecture tracking up to multi-second parameters. Feedback pathways are safety-limited deep within the DSP engine, allowing tail regeneration loops to run aggressively hot without forcing signal cascading failures.

MOTION ENGINE

A multi-target LFO modulation network. Instantly map parameters to delay timings, wet amplitude tiers, structural filtration matrices, or dedicated feedback EQ blocks to shift movement profiles from subtle drift to severe pitch modulation.

SPATIAL & WIDTH

Dedicated space configurations tracking stereo offset definitions and system crossfeed networks. The channel path orientation is specifically built around a listener-centric topology map for seamless tracking within modern immersive formats.

SPECTRAL TONE-SHAPING

Features integrated color tilt, high/low filtering boundaries, and a tunable resonant band nestled directly inside the feedback loop. Repeats actively mutate and evolve over time rather than simply dissipating into conventional degradation arrays.

DARKEN & DAMAGE

Darken continuously draws late-stage reflection tails into dense high-frequency shadows as decay routines execute. Conversely, Damage drives a hard saturation stage that spans cleanly from subtle tube warmth up to destructive clipped formats at extreme parameter thresholds.

GATE & CHOP

A rhythmic gating component driven by discrete MIDI trigger and retrigger modes. Ideal for shaping gated-reverb delay topologies, synchronized chop metrics, and transient stutters locked directly onto specific host session lines.
