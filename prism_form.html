PRISM FORM

Spectral Sound-Shaping Instrument

RETURN_TO_LAB STORE

PRISM FORM is a sampler built entirely around the spectrum. Drop in source audio blocks, isolate precision elements—by region, by frequency band, or freehand—and completely reshape what plays back. Powered by a polyphonic voice core and managed through a high-focus spectral matrix workspace, it maps raw captures into completely fluid textures.

Chassis currently undergoing bench testing. Initial builds are calibrated to universal macOS configurations (Apple Silicon & Intel) supporting VST3, Audio Unit, and Standalone layers.
