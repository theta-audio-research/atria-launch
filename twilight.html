TWILIGHT

Regenerative ambience for deep listening and deep design.

TWILIGHT is not a conventional reverb, delay, or drone plugin. It is a regenerative ambience instrument designed to generate, layer, destabilize, widen, sequence, and finish sound inside a single environment. At its core is THETA, a four-voice binaural engine built for sustained tone, harmonic interaction, and psychoacoustic motion. Each voice can stand alone or relate to the others through note behavior, beat interaction, interval structure, and linked movement. That lets TWILIGHT move from calm, centered drones to dense, shifting harmonic systems that feel alive from the inside out. With the Signal Lattice, each voice can become rhythmic, sequenced, and generative. Gate, pitch, probability, velocity, and swing turn the instrument from a held drone machine into a living pattern network. The heart of its widening character is DRIFT, a harmony-doubler designed for musical thickening. DRIFT can act as a unison expander, octave stacker, fifth spreader, or chordal harmonizer, with modes for major, minor, suspended, and cluster voicings. TWILIGHT also welcomes external sources via the V5 Sample Layer and THRU path, allowing it to work as a self-contained generative instrument or a transformative processor for incoming material. TWILIGHT is for the space between structure and emergence.
